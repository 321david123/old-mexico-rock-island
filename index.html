Who We Are

A family kitchen
on 45th Street.

Old Mexico is a family-run, neighborhood Mexican kitchen serving recipes passed down through the family. Step inside and you'll find a cozy dining room beneath a vintage pressed-tin ceiling — the kind of room that's been welcoming Quad Cities regulars for years.

The kitchen is known for its quesabirria tacos with rich consomé for dipping, daily pozole and menudo, hand-made tamales, and a breakfast of eggs and chorizo. Service is fast and friendly, portions are generous, prices are unbeatable — and the chips and salsa never stop coming.
